About Chocolate Hills

The Chocolate Hills are a geological formation in the Bohol province of the Philippines. They are a unique and stunning landscape that consists of approximately 1,268 perfectly cone-shaped hills.

During the dry season, the grass covering the hills dries up and turns brown, giving the hills the appearance of chocolate kisses. This natural wonder is a popular tourist attraction and is recognized as one of the country's national symbols.

Exploring the Hills

Experience the beauty of the Chocolate Hills by:

  • Chocolate Hills Complex: Visit the Chocolate Hills Complex for panoramic views and learn more about the geological formation.
  • Carmen View Deck: Head to Carmen View Deck for breathtaking views and photo opportunities of the Chocolate Hills.
  • Sagbayan Peak: Explore Sagbayan Peak, an elevated area offering stunning vistas of the hills and surrounding landscape.
  • Adventure Tours: Consider taking an adventure tour to hike or bike around the Chocolate Hills for a closer encounter with this natural wonder.
