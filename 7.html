About Mayon Volcano

Mayon, also known as Mount Mayon and Mayon Volcano, is an active stratovolcano located in the province of Albay in Bicol, Philippines. Renowned for its "perfect cone" due to its symmetric conical shape, Mayon is considered one of the most picturesque volcanoes in the world.

The volcano has a rich history of eruptions, contributing to the fertile volcanic soils surrounding its base. Mayon Volcano is a popular tourist spot, attracting visitors with its breathtaking views and natural allure.

Exploring the Volcano

Discover the wonders of Mayon Volcano by:

  • Lava Wall Viewing: Visit designated viewpoints to witness the majestic sight of the lava walls descending during past eruptions.
  • Climbing Adventure: For the adventurous, embark on a guided climb to explore Mayon's slopes and enjoy panoramic views from higher elevations.
  • Cagsawa Ruins: Explore the historical Cagsawa Ruins, the remnants of a town destroyed during the 1814 eruption, offering a unique perspective of Mayon's power.
  • Legazpi Boulevard: Visit Legazpi Boulevard for stunning waterfront views of Mayon Volcano against the backdrop of Albay Gulf.
