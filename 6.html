Welcome to Hundred Islands

Explore the beauty of Hundred Islands, a stunning archipelago in the Philippines.

About Hundred Islands

Hundred Islands National Park is the first Philippine national park and a protected area located in Alaminos, Pangasinan. The park features a group of 124 islands and islets, revealing breathtaking landscapes and diverse marine life.

Each island has its own unique charm, with some offering white sandy beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant coral reefs. The park is a haven for nature lovers, beach enthusiasts, and those seeking a peaceful getaway.

Exploring the Islands

Discover the wonders of Hundred Islands by:

  • Island Hopping: Embark on an island-hopping adventure to explore the different islands, each with its own distinct features and beauty.
  • Snorkeling and Diving: Dive into the clear waters to witness the vibrant marine life, colorful coral reefs, and unique underwater ecosystems.
  • Caving and Hiking: Explore the caves and hiking trails on some islands, offering panoramic views of the archipelago and surrounding waters.
  • Beach Relaxation: Unwind on the pristine beaches, enjoy the sun, and take in the tranquility of the natural surroundings.
