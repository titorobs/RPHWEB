About Puerto Princesa

Puerto Princesa is the capital city of the province of Palawan. Nestled in the western part of the Philippines, it boasts a unique blend of pristine natural beauty and cultural richness.

Home to the renowned Puerto Princesa Underground River, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, this city offers an immersive experience in lush landscapes and captivating history.

Popular Attractions

Discover the wonders of Puerto Princesa by visiting these attractions:

  • Puerto Princesa Underground River: Embark on a journey through a subterranean marvel, marveling at limestone formations and unique ecosystems.
  • Honda Bay: Relax on pristine beaches and explore crystal-clear waters in this breathtaking bay.
  • Palawan Wildlife Rescue and Conservation Center: Encounter diverse wildlife and contribute to conservation efforts.
  • Nagtabon Beach: Enjoy the sun and surf on this scenic beach, perfect for water activities and relaxation.
  • Mitra's Ranch: Revel in panoramic views of Puerto Princesa and enjoy outdoor activities in this scenic ranch.
  • Immaculate Conception Cathedral: Visit this historical and architectural gem, showcasing the city's cultural heritage.
  • Baker's Hill: Indulge in delightful treats and explore charming gardens at this popular attraction.
